University of Helsinki

A Taste of Finnish This online course is designed for students planning to come to study at the University of Helsinki for a term or two.
Do not try to compare Finnish with your mother tongue (unless it happens to be Estonian, which is closely related to Finnish) Instead, empty your mind and take Finnish as it comes –  a different but an exciting language! Welcome to study Finnish!

Kielitohtori

Kielitohtori
Kielitohtori.fi:ssä voit saada Kielitohtorilta neuvoja suomen kieltä koskevissa kielenhuoltoasioissa.
Kielitohtori on käännöstoimisto Translatumin tuottama ilmaispalvelu, joka vastaa visaisiin termikysymyksiisi ja ratkoo hankalat kielenhuolto-ongelmat.

Finnish for Immigrants

Finnish for Immigrants - Free of Charge Course Package
Finnish for Immigrants helps you master the basics of the Finnish language. The course package includes two elementary level courses (Finnish 1 and Finnish 2) as well as an introduction to verb conjugation in present tense.
Completing the courses takes about 15 hours on average. This package is a perfect solution if you are a foreigner living in Finland and your language skills are elementary – it covers the most essential vocabulary and expressions that you will encounter in everyday life.
